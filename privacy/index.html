Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Overview

KENSO does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data processing occurs locally on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no cloud storage.

Data Processing

All statistical analyses, data imports, and exports are performed entirely on your local machine. Your research data never leaves your computer.

License Activation

When you enter a license key, KENSO validates it locally against a cryptographic signature. No online verification or account registration is required.

Update Checks

KENSO periodically checks for new versions by requesting a small version file from GitHub. No personal or usage data is transmitted during this process.

Payments

License purchases are processed by Polar (polar.sh), a third-party payment provider. KENSO does not have access to your payment details. Please refer to Polar's privacy policy for details on payment data handling.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@kenso.shop.

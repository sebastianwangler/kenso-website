First launch: Windows SmartScreen

On first launch, Windows may show a blue dialog titled "Windows protected your PC". Simply follow these steps:

  1. 1 Click More info (small blue link near the top of the dialog)
  2. 2 Click Run anyway
  3. 3 KENSO will open — Windows remembers your choice for future starts

Why does this happen?

Microsoft SmartScreen warns about any application that lacks an Extended Validation code-signing certificate. KENSO is built by an independent developer and currently ships without that certificate. The app is safe to run — it runs entirely on your PC and your data never leaves the machine.

Troubleshooting

  • Right-click KENSO.exe → Properties → tick Unblock (if shown) → OK
  • If your company blocks installers from unknown publishers, ask IT to whitelist KENSO
